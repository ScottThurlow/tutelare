## The problem in one paragraph

Code review was designed for the volume and tempo of human authorship. It assumed
that every line entering the codebase had passed through a human's working memory.
That assumption no longer holds at AI-generation volume. Teams either drown trying
to maintain the old discipline, or quietly relax it and accept whatever risk follows.
